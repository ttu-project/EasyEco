EasyEco Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

EasyEco is an educational mobile app that helps users learn about eco-friendly habits and related product information. This policy explains what information EasyEco may collect and how that information is used.

Information We Collect

When you sign in, EasyEco may collect basic account information such as your name, email address, and profile information provided by the login provider. The app may also store information you submit while using EasyEco features.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to create and manage your account, provide app features, improve the app experience, and support authentication through providers such as Google or Facebook.

Sharing

EasyEco does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the app, comply with legal requests, or protect the app and its users.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your EasyEco account data by following the instructions on our Data Deletion Instructions page.
