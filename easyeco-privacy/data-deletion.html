EasyEco Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 6, 2026

EasyEco users can request deletion of personal data associated with their account at any time.

How To Request Deletion

Send an email to ingyin2031@gmail.com with the subject line "EasyEco Data Deletion Request".

In your email, include the email address or login provider account used for EasyEco so we can identify the account data to delete.

What Will Be Deleted

We will delete personal account information stored by EasyEco, including account profile information and app data connected to your account, unless retention is required by law or needed for security records.

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are reviewed and processed within a reasonable period after we receive enough information to verify the account.
